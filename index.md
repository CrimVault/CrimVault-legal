# Privacy Policy for CrimVault

<_Last updated: May 2, 2026_>

CrimVault is an educational mobile application for criminology licensure review, neuro-style self-review, and government entrance exam preparation such as NAPOLCOM, FOE, POE, and Civil Service.

This policy explains what information is stored, how it is used, and what third-party services may process information.

---

## 1. Offline-First App

CrimVault is designed as an offline-first review app. We do not operate an account server for study activity, exam scores, reviewer profiles, or progress records.

Most app data stays on your device, including:

- local reviewer profiles
- local login PIN data
- profile name, school, exam date, avatar, and optional profile image path
- practice, mock exam, neuro exam, service exam, and theoretical lesson progress
- bookmarks, wrong-answer history, flashcard scheduling, studied lesson records, and achievements
- preferences such as text size, trial state, and app settings

---

## 2. Local Login and Security

CrimVault uses local reviewer profiles for convenience on a shared device.

- Reviewer profiles are stored only on your device.
- PIN/password hashes and related security values are stored using device secure storage where available.
- PINs are not sent to us.
- We do not use Google, Facebook, or other third-party sign-in providers.

Because this data is local, anyone with access to your unlocked device may be able to access the app. Please protect your device with a passcode, biometrics, or another device-level lock.

---

## 3. Profile Images and Device Files

If you choose to add a profile image, CrimVault may ask permission to select an image from your device.

CrimVault stores the selected image path locally so it can display the profile image. The image is not uploaded to us. You can remove the profile image from your profile settings.

---

## 4. Purchases and Premium Access

CrimVault offers optional premium features through in-app purchases.

Payments are processed by the app store provider, such as Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your credit card number, payment credentials, or full billing details.

The app may store a local premium entitlement flag and trial start date on your device so premium access can be restored inside the app.

Store providers may process purchase, tax, device, and account information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

---

## 5. External Links

CrimVault may open external links, such as this Privacy Policy page, in your browser or another app. When you open an external link, the destination website or app may receive information such as your IP address, browser/device information, or referrer data according to its own policies.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your study data, reviewer profile data, exam results, or local progress records.

We do not share local app data with advertisers or data brokers.

Limited information may be processed by third-party platform providers only when needed for app distribution, purchases, crash/system services provided by the operating system, or external links you choose to open.

---

## 7. Data Storage and Retention

CrimVault stores app data locally on your device using local storage and secure storage.

Data remains on your device until you delete it, reset progress, delete a reviewer profile, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Some operating systems or device backup services may back up app data depending on your device settings. Those backups are controlled by your operating system or account provider, not by CrimVault.

---

## 8. Data Deletion

You can remove local CrimVault data by:

- deleting a reviewer profile inside the app
- resetting progress inside the app
- removing a profile image inside the app
- clearing app data in device settings
- uninstalling the app

Because study data is stored locally and not on our servers, we cannot remotely recover or delete data from your device.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

CrimVault is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has used the app and wants local data removed, they can delete the reviewer profile, clear app data, or uninstall the app from the device.

---

## 10. Educational Disclaimer

CrimVault is for educational and review purposes only.

- It is not affiliated with PRC, CHED, NAPOLCOM, CSC, BFP, BJMP, PNP, or any government agency.
- It is not an official examination provider.
- Neuro-style content is for educational self-review only and is not a medical, psychological, employment, or official fitness determination.
- Always verify current exam requirements, laws, schedules, and official policies with the appropriate official source.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new revision date.
