# Privacy Policy for CrimVault

Last updated: May 5, 2026

CrimVault is an educational mobile application for criminology licensure review. The current Android-first release focuses on CLE theory lessons, practice questions, mock exams, flashcards, weakness tracking, and progress analytics.

This policy explains what information is stored, how it is used, and what third-party services may process information.

---

## 1. Offline-First App

CrimVault is designed as an offline-first review app. We do not operate an account server for study activity, exam scores, or progress records.

Most app data stays on your device, including:

- theoretical lesson progress
- practice and mock exam progress
- answered question history
- correct answer history
- subject accuracy and weak subject records
- flashcard study state
- study streak and study time estimates
- app settings

---

## 2. Local Storage

CrimVault does not require account registration in the current Android-first release.

- Study progress is stored only on your device.
- No PINs, passwords, or account credentials are required by this release.
- We do not use Google, Facebook, or other third-party sign-in providers.

Because this data is local, anyone with access to your unlocked device may be able to view your study progress. Please protect your device with a passcode, biometrics, or another device-level lock.

---

## 3. Device Files

The current Android-first release does not require photo, camera, contacts, microphone, location, Bluetooth, or file storage permissions for its core study features.

---

## 4. Purchases and Premium Access

The current Android-first release does not sell digital goods or unlock premium content through payment.

Premium is shown only as a preview of planned future upgrades. If paid digital content is enabled in a future release, purchases must use the official platform billing system and this policy will be updated before release.

---

## 5. External Links

CrimVault may open external links, such as this Privacy Policy page, in your browser or another app. When you open an external link, the destination website or app may receive information such as your IP address, browser/device information, or referrer data according to its own policies.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your study data, exam results, or local progress records.

We do not share local app data with advertisers or data brokers.

Limited information may be processed by third-party platform providers only when needed for app distribution, operating-system services, or external links you choose to open.

---

## 7. Data Storage and Retention

CrimVault stores app data locally on your device using local storage.

Data remains on your device until you reset progress, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Some operating systems or device backup services may back up app data depending on your device settings. Those backups are controlled by your operating system or account provider, not by CrimVault.

---

## 8. Data Deletion

You can remove local CrimVault data by:

- resetting progress inside the app
- clearing app data in device settings
- uninstalling the app

Because study data is stored locally and not on our servers, we cannot remotely recover or delete data from your device.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

CrimVault is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has used the app and wants local data removed, they can clear app data or uninstall the app from the device.

---

## 10. Educational Disclaimer

CrimVault is for educational and review purposes only.

- It is not affiliated with PRC, CHED, NAPOLCOM, CSC, BFP, BJMP, PNP, or any government agency.
- It is not an official examination provider.
- Always verify current exam requirements, laws, schedules, and official policies with the appropriate official source.
